Quick Join with Group Code

You don't need to create an account upfront to join a Secret Santa group. Get the group code and password from your organizer, then join instantly. If you don't have an account yet, you'll create a simple one during the join process - it only takes a minute.

How to Join Without Pre-Signup

  1. Get Your Group Code: Your organizer will share a group code and password (via email, text, or QR code)
  2. Click "Join a Group": Go to our site and click "Join a Group" on the dashboard
  3. Enter Code & Password: Type in the group code and password your organizer provided
  4. Quick Account Creation: If you don't have an account, you'll create one right there (name, email, password - that's it!)
  5. You're In! Once joined, you can add your wishlist and wait for the draw

Why This Approach Works Better

  • ✅ No Pre-Signup Required: Join when you're ready, not before
  • ✅ Quick Account Creation: Simple signup process when you join
  • ✅ Group Code Access: Easy sharing via QR code, link, or text
  • ✅ Minimal Friction: Get people into groups quickly
  • ✅ Secure: Group password protects your exchange

For Organizers: Making It Easy for Participants

As an organizer, you can make joining easy for your participants by sharing the group code in multiple ways:

  • QR Code: Share a QR code that participants can scan with their phone
  • Direct Link: Send a link that takes participants directly to the join page
  • Group Code & Password: Share via email, text, or messaging app
  • Email Invites: Send email invitations that include the join information

Participants don't need to create accounts ahead of time - they can join when they receive your invitation and create their account during the join process.

Quick Answers About Joining Without Signup

Can I join a Secret Santa group without creating an account? To join a group, you'll need a simple account, but you can create it quickly when joining with a group code. The organizer creates the group and shares a code, then participants create accounts when they join.

How do I join with just a group code? Get the group code and password from your organizer, then click 'Join a Group' on our site. Enter the code and password, and you'll be prompted to create a quick account if you don't have one.

Do I need to verify my email to join? Email verification depends on the organizer's settings. In many cases, you can join and participate immediately after creating your account.

What if I already have an account? If you already have an account, just sign in and use the group code to join. No need to create a new account.
