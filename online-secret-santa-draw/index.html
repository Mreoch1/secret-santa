Draw Secret Santa Names Online in Seconds

Forget paper slips, hat draws, or complicated spreadsheets. Our online Secret Santa draw makes it easy to randomly assign gift recipients with just one click. Perfect for remote teams, distributed families, or anyone who wants a modern, digital solution.

The online draw happens instantly, automatically avoids self-matches and blocked pairs, and sends each participant their assignment via email. No need to gather everyone in the same room.

How the Online Draw Works

  1. Create Your Group: Set up your Secret Santa group online with a few clicks
  2. Invite Participants: Share a link, QR code, or group code - participants join from anywhere
  3. Click to Draw: One button automatically draws names and sends assignments via email
  4. Everyone Gets Notified: Each participant receives their Secret Santa assignment instantly

Benefits of an Online Secret Santa Draw

  • ✅ Works Remotely: No need to be in the same room - perfect for remote teams
  • ✅ Instant Results: Draw names and send assignments in seconds
  • ✅ Automatic Matching: Smart algorithm prevents self-draws and blocked pairs
  • ✅ Email Notifications: Everyone gets their assignment automatically
  • ✅ Undo & Redraw: Made a mistake? Undo and redraw anytime
  • ✅ Secure & Private: Each person only sees their own assignment

Perfect for Remote Teams and Distributed Groups

The online Secret Santa draw is perfect for modern workplaces and families spread across different locations. Quick Answers About Online Draws

How does the online Secret Santa draw work? Create a group online, invite participants, and click one button to automatically draw names. Everyone receives their assignment instantly via email.

Do I need to be in the same room to draw names? No, the online draw works completely remotely. Participants can join from anywhere and receive their assignments via email.

Can I undo and redraw names if needed? Yes, organizers can undo and rerun the online draw at any time if someone joins late or you need to adjust settings.

Is the online draw secure and private? Yes, each participant only sees their own assignment. The organizer can see the full list, but participants' assignments remain private.
