Quick answers about family Secret Santa

What if our family is spread across different cities? Holiday Draw Names works great for remote families and sends assignments by email so everyone can take part in the family secret santa draw.

Can we reuse the same family group every year? Yes, simply update the participant list and rerun the holiday name draw when you are ready for this year's exchange.

How do we keep surprises from being spoiled? Only the organizer sees the full list of who has who, while each family member sees just their own assignment.
