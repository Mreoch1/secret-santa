Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 1, 2025

1. Introduction

Holiday Draw Names ("we," "our," or "us") operates holidaydrawnames.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Secret Santa gift exchange service.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password (encrypted).

Group Information: We store information about the Secret Santa groups you create or join, including group codes and participant lists.

Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage data to improve our service.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Secret Santa service
  • Send you email notifications about your Secret Santa assignments
  • Communicate with you about your account
  • Improve our service and user experience

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored using Supabase, a trusted database provider. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your information.

Passwords are encrypted and never stored in plain text.

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Your Secret Santa assignment is only visible to you.

We only share information when required by law or to protect our rights.

6. Cookies

We use cookies to maintain your login session and improve user experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect functionality.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Opt-out of email communications

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via email or website notice.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@holidaydrawnames.com
